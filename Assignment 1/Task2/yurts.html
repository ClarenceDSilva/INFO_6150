The Yurts at Pacific Trails

What is a yurt?
Our luxury yurts are permanent struuctures four feet off the ground. Each yurt has canvas walls, a wooden floor, and a roof dome that can be opened.
How are the yurts furnished?
Each yurt is furnished with a queen-size bed with down quilt and gas-fired stove.The luxury camping experience also includes electicity and a sink with hot and cold running water.Shower and restroom facilities are location in the lodge
What should I bring?
Bring a sense of adventure and sometime to relax! Most guests also pack comfortable walking and plan to dress for changing weather with layers of clothing
